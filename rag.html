Retrieval Augmentation and Vector Embeddings

  1. Home

Business Need
    Develop a chatbot that deliver precise answers to inquiries from international clients regarding specific products they were interested in purchasing from the company.
The problem
    Large Language Models (LLMs) have a data freshness problem. Even some of the most powerful models, like GPT-4, have no idea about recent events. When prompts are submitted to OpenAI, the responses, although grammatically correct, may not be accurate due to a phenomenon known as hallucination, highlighting a significant challenge in ensuring data freshness. This risk of providing inaccurate information to customers may significantly impact the reputation of the company and/or lead to other detrimental consequences.
The proposed and implemented solution
    Utilizing the architecture outlined below, I endeavored to create a Vector DB that encompasses the company's information. This process involved crawling URLs from the company's website, retrieving internal data via specific APIs, extracting information from PDFs detailing the service offerings and product manuals, then chunking and processing this data through an embedding model before uploading it into the Vector DB using the appropriate API.
The results
    As a result, by querying the Vector DB for precise answers and then submitting these queries to OpenAI for grammatical refinement, customers received responses that were both accurate and grammatically correct. The tangible impact of this strategy led to a remarkable 28% increase in sales and a substantial improvement in customer retention KPIs by 12%.
